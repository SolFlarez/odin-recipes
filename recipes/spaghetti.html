Mom's Spaghetti

Description

This is the world famous Mom's Spaghetti from famous rap song "Lose Yourself" by Eminem. All credit is given to Mr. Nem's mother as the the original creator of this delicious recipe.

Ingredients

  • Sauce
  • noodles
  • ground beef
  • spices n such

Steps

  1. boil water - add noodles
  2. brown beef in pan
  3. add sauce to pan
  4. add noodles to pan
  5. season with herbs and spices
  6. eat the food
